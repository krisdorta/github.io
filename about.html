Who am I?

My name is Kristhyan Dorta, I am an Advertising major and this is my story. I was born and raised in Venezuela and moved to the US in 2010. I came to this country looking for a better future because of the situation back home.

 

What is my educational background?

I have a bachelor's degree in Business Administration from NSU, an MBA from the same Univerisity and a Master in Sports Marketing from the Universidad Europea of Madrid.

 

What are my passions?

I have a deep passion for marketing, sales, brand development and anything related to that area. My favourite adobe program is Illustrator and I love going outdoors and photographing nature. I have worked in several places so far.

 

A little about my professional experience...

My first job was as Marketing Assistant for a company that owned a restaurant and a spa, after that I did an internship, in Madrid, at Mutua Madrid Open and also at Real Madrid for two months. Currently I am working for a restaurant as the Social Media Manager. I love being creative and developing brands to their full potential, I can't wait to graduate to finally go out there and demonstrate what I am capable of.
